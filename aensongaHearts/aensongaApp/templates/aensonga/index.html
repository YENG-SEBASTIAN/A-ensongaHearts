Learn About Us

A Ghanaian non-profit charity organization

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision

A-ensonga Hearts is a charity organization in Ghana focused on supporting needy children, particularly in the realm of early childhood education. Officially established in January 2020 and registered as a non-governmental, non-profit organization in July 2021, the group aims to address the needs of vulnerable children. Over the past two years, A-ensonga Hearts has collaborated with other organizations to provide educational support for children in Ghana. Their mission is rooted in the belief that education is a transformative tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. The organization advocates for a redefined approach to philanthropy, emphasizing dynamic community mobilization to address challenges hindering the potential of young, vulnerable Ghanaian children. Their slogan, "Lighten the World with Love," reflects their commitment to bringing joy and hope to children facing challenging circumstances.

  • Identifying unique challenges confronting children that inhibit their potential for development.
  • Mobilizing support to provide relief for vulnerable children.
  • Responding to the educational needs of children in vulnerable conditions.
  • To be a leader in the provision of support to vulnerable in Ghana especially in the educational sector in Ghana in the next 20 years

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Clean Environment Campaign

The Clean Environment Campaign is dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability and raising awareness about the importance of a clean and healthy environment.

Clean Water

The Clean Water Initiative is committed to providing sustainable access to clean water, strives to improve the health and well-being of communities through water sanitation projects.

Primary Education

A-EnsongaHearts Educational Program is dedicated to ensuring inclusive and quality education for all, particularly in underserved communities, to empower children with the foundational knowledge and skills for a brighter future.

Mentorship

The Mentorship Program is designed to empower individuals by fostering meaningful connections and providing guidance to unlock their full potential and navigate personal and professional development

Social Care

It is dedicated to enhancing the well-being and social integration of vulnerable individuals and communities through a range of support services and programs.
